Resumé

Summary

Adam is a computer technology and design professional of 9 years, leading award-winning digital experiences that have helped transform how people interact with brands and how businesses engage with their audience.

His breadth of experience ranges from software engineering and experience design to business development and team leadership, which allows him to take projects from blank-slate ideation through to execution, launch, and beyond. Adam thrives in fast-paced entrepreneurial environments and also tight-knit agile product teams. He is a strong and compassionate leader who fosters a team dynamic based on hard work, openness, and support.

Adam has worked with brands like Nike, Sony, Universal Music, Pepsi, and RBC and artists like Ariana Grande, Drake, and Bruno Mars. He is a recipient of a Cannes Lion and two Clios. His work has been featured in Bloomberg News, Wired, TechCrunch, and Billboard.
